# BrightSmile Dental Clinic — Patient Policies

## Cancellation and rescheduling policy
We kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule an
appointment. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a $30 fee.
You can reschedule through this assistant or by contacting the clinic.

## Late arrivals
If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule your
appointment to avoid disrupting other patients.

## Refunds
Deposits for treatment plans are refundable up to 48 hours before the scheduled
treatment. Payments for completed treatments are non-refundable.

## Privacy and your data
We handle your personal information in line with applicable privacy laws. We do
not share your data with third parties without your consent, except where
required by law. This demo assistant does not store real patient data.

## Complaints
We take feedback seriously. If you are unhappy with any aspect of your care,
please let us know and a member of our team will follow up with you. Complaints
are logged and reviewed by our practice manager.

## Children and guardians
Patients under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian for their
first visit and for any treatment.
