# BrightSmile Dental Clinic — Services

## General dentistry
Routine check-ups, professional cleaning, tooth-coloured fillings, and
extractions. We recommend a check-up every six months.

## Orthodontics
Teeth-straightening options including traditional braces and clear aligners.
Treatment begins with an orthodontic consultation to assess suitability.

## Teeth whitening
Professional in-clinic whitening and take-home whitening kits for a brighter
smile. Whitening is cosmetic and suitability is confirmed at a short assessment.

## Dental implants
Replacement of missing teeth with durable dental implants. Treatment starts with
an implant consultation and imaging.

## Emergency dental care
Same-day emergency assessments for pain, swelling, bleeding, or dental trauma.
Please contact the clinic directly for emergencies. This assistant cannot
diagnose or treat medical conditions; it only records a human-review request when
a completed action confirms one.
