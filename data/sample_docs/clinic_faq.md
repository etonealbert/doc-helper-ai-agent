# BrightSmile Dental Clinic — Frequently Asked Questions

## Opening hours
We are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on public
holidays. Saturday visits are available for emergencies only, by prior
arrangement.

## Location and parking
BrightSmile Dental Clinic is located at 120 Maple Street, Riverton. Free patient
parking is available at the rear of the building, accessible from Oak Lane.

## How do I book an appointment?
You can request an appointment through this assistant, by phone, or through our
website. New patients are asked to arrive 10 minutes early to complete a short
intake form.

## Do you accept new patients?
Yes, we are currently accepting new patients for general dentistry, orthodontics,
teeth whitening, and dental implants.

## What should I do in a dental emergency?
If you are experiencing severe pain, heavy bleeding, facial swelling, a fever, or
have had a tooth knocked out, please contact the clinic immediately. If it is a
life-threatening emergency, call your local emergency services. This assistant
cannot provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice.

## Do you send appointment reminders?
Yes. We send a reminder by email or SMS 48 hours before your appointment. You can
choose your preferred contact method at your first visit.
